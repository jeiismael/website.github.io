J&J Clouds

We are a homebased shop located in Cainta, Rizal. J&J Clouds is an official distributor of Black Elite Products. We started venturing in the vape industry during the 4th quarter of 2022. We are open to resellers and we deliver to any part of the country. Our customers from Luzon and all the way up to the corners of Sulu and Zamboanga City, located in Mindanao, are able to vouch for the professionalism and integrity of our transactions.

Within the year, we are aiming to establish a physical store that would cater our customers locally. As soon as our establishment would come to fruition, J&J Clouds will be expanding with more products from various manufacturers and brands.

Our company's ultimate goal is to establish several stores and be the number one distributor of disposable vape within major cities across the country such as Cebu, Davao, Zamboanga City, and Metro Manila.
